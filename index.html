Frequently asked questions

How does it work?

Once everyone is seated, the host will ring a bell. Each date is six minutes long. After 5 minutes, there's a "warning" bell so you can wrap up your conversations. When the 6 minutes is up, you'll hear the bell again. That means it's time for the guys get up and move to the next table.

Is there an age range?

Yes. This event is for ages 25 to 35.

What if I click with someone and want to go on another date with them?

After the event, we send you pictures of everyone you went on a date with. Reply with the names of the people you want to see again. If somebody liked you too, we'll send both of you each other's phone numbers. You take it from there.

How do I refer friends to get my second free drink?

After you buy your ticket, you recieve a confirmation text. Reply to this text by saying, "My friend [John] referred me." Both of you will get two free drinks.

What if I have a horrible time?

Then we would feel really really bad. We'll give you your money back immediately and offer you a $10 gift card to your favorite place to eat. All you have to do is text us and say, "I had a horrible time and want my money back please." Don't be shy.

What if I can't hold a conversation for 6 minutes?

A lot of people worry about this. Afterwards, they realize how fast 6 minutes goes by. But don't worry, there will be fun games and conversation starters at each table.

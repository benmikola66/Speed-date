About

There's a huge gap in the dating industry.

Millions of us are looking for a relationship, but we don't know where to start.

Once we leave school, meeting people takes a lot more effort. Most of us just throw shit at a wall until it sticks.

We download the dating apps. We go to the bars. We join a coed sports team.

But are those really the best answers we can come up with?

Arrow didn't start because we think speed dating is the solution.

We just think we can figure out a better way. We're starting small, and will constantly be inventing and improving.
